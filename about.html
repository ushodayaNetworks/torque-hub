About TorqueHub

Our Story

Founded in 2023, TorqueHub was created with a simple mission: to make buying and selling used cars transparent, straightforward, and stress-free.

We recognized that the traditional used car market was filled with uncertainties, hidden fees, and complicated processes that left buyers and sellers frustrated. That's why we built TorqueHub as a direct connection between car owners and prospective buyers.

Today, TorqueHub serves thousands of customers nationwide, helping them find their perfect vehicles or sell their cars quickly and at fair prices.
